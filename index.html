About

Hey, there! I'm Ren, a Brazilian designer who fell in love with programming and now thrives where these two fields meet: web design and development! My passion is creating modern websites for creatives, from authors to bloggers to artists, and helping them spread the word about their work, find their audience and showcase their skills. Welcome!

Services

Wordpress

A Wordpress powered website is perfect for those looking for a blog or landing page that is easy to update and incredibly friendly to beginners.

Simple website

Need a hub for all your info? A website you won't need to update frequently after it is done? Then a static website is perfect for you!

Web App

Need something more complex? With login/register functionality or maybe a database? Then you need a modern web app!

Each project is different and I'm always open to discussing my rates, but as a start, static websites with five or less pages cost $150. Wordpress themes with five or less pages cost $250. Web apps' prices vary a lot depending on complexity (is a database necessary? Login/register functionality? etc), but $300 is the minimum.

For all services additional pages cost $15-$20 each depending on complexity. For wordpress themes, custom post types also cost extra, as do pages with different functionality for web apps, depending on complexity as well.

All payment is done through Paypal. Half is paid upfront, half when the project is complete. An e-mail will be sent to you with an estimated schedule for delivery of initial designs, as well as Trello board so you can track my progress on building your product.
